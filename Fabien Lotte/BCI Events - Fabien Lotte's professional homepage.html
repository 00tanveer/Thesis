BCI Events

Here is a list of upcoming conferences and events that are related to Brain-Computer Interfaces to some extent.
Typically, for conferences, this means BCI is mentioned in the conference scope or that this conference is known to present a number of BCI papers each year.
I will try to maintain this list updated.
Conferences and events marked as "XX" are those with a large focus on BCI and as such will most probably have more content about BCI than the other ones.
Don't hesitate to contact me if you are aware of a conference or event that should be listed here but is missing!

You will also find BCI-related events on the BCI Society Website : http://bcisociety.org/events/

Call for papers dedicated to BCI for special issues of journals can be found on a separate web page: journal special issues related to BCI.
